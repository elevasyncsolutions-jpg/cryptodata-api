FAQ
Questions? Answered.

Everything you need to know about CryptoBoss.

How do I get started?+
Click "Get Free API Key" to generate a key instantly with a $1 credit. No email or signup required. Then call any endpoint with the x-api-key header.
What does "post-paid" mean?+
You get a free $1 credit upfront. When it runs out, you send $1 USDC to our Solana wallet and your credit resets. You only pay after you use — no prepayment or subscription required.
How do I pay?+
Send $1 USDC to DEXbxpDbbj5AnZSqfAhuftvjrtXwjoWW1PgrxmVjuZef on Solana mainnet. Then POST to /api/pay with your key and transaction signature.
How much does each call cost?+
Prices range from $0.005 (gas, sentiment) to $0.05 (meme analysis). Most endpoints are $0.01. Your $1 credit covers roughly 100–200 calls depending on the endpoints used.
Which AI agents are supported?+
Any agent that supports the Model Context Protocol (MCP): Claude Desktop, Cursor, Cline, Windsurf, and others. Just add the MCP server URL and 42+ tools are auto-discovered.
What data sources do you use?+
We aggregate from CoinGecko (prices, market data), DeFiLlama (yields, TVL), DexScreener (meme tokens), Alternative.me (Fear & Greed), and Etherscan (on-chain data).
Do you have a CLI?+
Yes! Install npm install -g cryptoboss-cli for a terminal-friendly way to interact with CryptoBoss. Commands: register, price, gas, meme-scan, and more.
